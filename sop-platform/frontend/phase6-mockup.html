My recommendation: Option A

It builds on the existing sop.$id.procedure.tsx layout with minimal disruption. The steps sidebar already exists. The video replaces the current screenshot placeholder. Transcript + callouts slide in below the video as a tabbed or split bottom panel. This is the fastest path to a working UI and feels natural — the user focuses on one step at a time, with its clip playing above and the relevant transcript lines highlighted below.

Question for you: Do you want the video to always show the step clip (short per-step MP4), or should users also be able to switch to the full original video and scrub through it?
